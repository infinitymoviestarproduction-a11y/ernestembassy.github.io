Full Biography of Damian Anielozie Okafor

Founder & CEO, Infinity Movie Star Production
Founder, Ernest Embassy Travels

Infinity Movie Star Production founder and Chief Executive Officer, Damian Anielozie Okafor, is a Nigerian filmmaker, scriptwriter, entrepreneur, educator, and travel consultant with a passion for transforming ideas into impactful stories and life-changing opportunities.

Born into the family of Late Luke Nkwoemeka Okafor Odowa and Mrs. Okwuchukwu Roseline Okafor Odowa, Damian is the sixth child in his family. From an early age, he demonstrated a deep interest in creativity, leadership, education, and entrepreneurship.

Damian earned his Nigeria Certificate in Education (NCE) from Enugu State College of Education (Technical) and became a registered member of the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria. He began his teaching career in 2007 and later worked in several sectors including education, logistics, manufacturing, private security, and hospitality. These experiences shaped his broad understanding of people, business, and storytelling.

In 2013, he founded Infinity Movie Star Production with the vision of producing compelling films that entertain, educate, and inspire audiences around the world. The company was formally registered in 2017 and has grown into a platform for film production, script development, talent discovery, and practical training for aspiring actors and filmmakers.

Damian is the writer of ambitious film projects such as Official Card and Round Table, alongside other titles including Playground, Land of Edomita, Heaven's Gate, Twins Blood, and Girls Go Wild. His storytelling often explores themes of justice, corruption, faith, family, and redemption.

Beyond filmmaking, Damian is also the founder of Ernest Embassy Travels, a travel consultancy that helps students, professionals, and families secure visas and pursue opportunities abroad for study, tourism, and employment.

A former football and volleyball enthusiast, Damian brings discipline, teamwork, and perseverance to every project. His personal motto, "Creating stories that live beyond the screen," reflects his commitment to producing meaningful content and building ventures that positively impact lives.

Today, Damian continues to expand his creative and business endeavors, seeking collaborations with investors, artists, and production partners across Nigeria, Zimbabwe, Malawi, and beyond.

Our Story

Everything started in the classroom in 2007. As a teacher, Damian saw how many students and families struggled with study abroad applications and visa processes. That led to the birth of Ernest Embassy Travels.

At the same time, Damian's passion for storytelling and movies grew into Infinity Movie Star Production, registered with the CAC on June 21, 2017 (Certificate No: BN2517233), dedicated to producing quality films and training aspiring filmmakers.
