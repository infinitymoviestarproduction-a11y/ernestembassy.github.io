About Ernest Embassy Travels & Infinity Movie Star Production

Global Travel • Film Production • Education Consulting • Online Tutoring

Who We Are

Ernest Embassy Travels and Infinity Movie Star Production operate as one vision led by one person — Damian Anielozie Okafor, a teacher, visa consultant, film producer, and online tutor.

We are not a large agency with anonymous staff. We are a hands-on service provider where you deal directly with the founder.

Our Story

Everything started in the classroom in 2014. As a teacher, Damian saw how many students and families struggled with study abroad applications and visa processes. That led to the birth of Ernest Embassy Travels.

At the same time, Damian's passion for storytelling and movies grew into Infinity Movie Star Production, registered with the CAC on June 21, 2017 (Certificate No: BN2517233), dedicated to producing quality films and training aspiring filmmakers.

Our Divisions

✈ Ernest Embassy Travels

We specialize in visa processing, travel consultation, flight guidance, and relocation support for students, tourists, and workers.

🎬 Infinity Movie Star Production

We create high-quality films, scripts, and entertainment content, including movie production, directing, editing, and storytelling. We also offer hands-on training for aspiring actors, directors, and crew members.

🎓 Educational Consulting

We guide students in selecting schools abroad, applying for admission, and securing student visas for international education opportunities.

💻 Online Tutoring

Damian personally teaches English Language, Digital Technology, Igbo for diaspora students, and coordinates science exam preparation (Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics for WAEC, NECO, JAMB) via WhatsApp platform. Adult literacy also available.
