🎬 Infinity Movie Star Production

Professional Film Production, International Collaboration & Hands-On Training

Infinity Movie Star – Where Every Story Begins With You.

Our Mission

"Creating stories that live beyond the screen."

Infinity Movie Star Production is a full-service film production company founded by Damian Anielozie Okafor, a Nigerian screenwriter, director, producer, and film trainer. We create compelling stories that inspire, entertain, and address real social issues while building global partnerships across Africa, Europe, and the United States.

Through strategic collaborations with Never Matewere of Zomba Film Production and Emmanuel Fragrance Francisque of Ashanti Media Logistics, we provide international production support, talent sourcing, logistics, and training for projects intended for global audiences.
