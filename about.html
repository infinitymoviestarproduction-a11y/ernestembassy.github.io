About Ernest Embassy Travels

Who We Are

Ernest Embassy Travels is a trusted international service company focused on helping individuals achieve global opportunities through travel, education, and professional development.

We operate as a multi-division organization combining travel consultancy, film production, and educational advisory services under one brand.

Our Divisions

✈ Ernest Embassy Travels

We specialize in visa processing, travel consultation, flight guidance, and relocation support for students, tourists, and workers.

🎬 Infinity Movie Star Production

We create high-quality films, scripts, and entertainment content, including movie production, directing, editing, and storytelling.

🎓 Educational Consulting

We guide students in selecting schools abroad, applying for admission, and securing student visas for international education opportunities.

Our Mission

To connect people to global opportunities through travel, education, and creative entertainment services.
